About Princeton

Princeton Science School .

Princeton Science School-Home of Young Achievers believes that the quality of education students receive today will determine what they will be in the future. In this constantly changing digital world, it is imperative for the school to have foresight of the challenges that the future brings. The school also believes that education, to be of superior level, must be unadulterated service to students and their families and not a product of commercialism. The primary purpose of an educational institution is to make difference in the lives of the students and that monetary rewards only come as a consequence of providing quality education.
