About Princeton

Mission

Princeton Science School aims to provide the highest level of education focusing on the importance of English and Math as a tool in preparing young minds in conquering tough challenges in the future. It also aspires to imbue in the children the values of integrity, humility, and sacrifice for them to grow as socially responsible members of society.


Vision

The Vision of Princeton Science School is to be a topnotch educational institution in the country by producing dynamic and high caliber graduates. Through advanced and world-class pedagogical strategies, the schoool is gearing up to be the premier provider of unparalleled academic competence in Math and Science. As an English-oriented school, it envisions learners and leaders par excellence who can outclass the rigors in the Global arena for seamless service to humanity God Almighty.
